River Parks

It's the perfect place to relax. Here, you'll find green areas with benches for picnics and spending time with friends and family. Arví Park

The Arví Park in Medellín is an incredible place to connect with nature and enjoy outdoor activities. You can immerse yourself in the beauty of nature, take part in thrilling outdoor adventures, and experience the peace of this unique destination. It's a perfect place to escape the routine and enjoy nature in your free time!

  • What to do?
  • ➣ Hiking.
  • ➣ Camping.
  • ➣ Cycling.
  • ➣ Have picnics and barbecues.
  • ➣ Swim in natural pools.

Botanical Garden

The Botanical Garden is an oasis of beauty right in the heart of the city. Here, you can explore gardens filled with amazing plants and sculptures. Walk along shaded paths that lead to orchid and cactus gardens. Discover the incredible diversity of the region, and don't miss the Butterfly Pavilion. This place allows you to relax, learn about the local nature, and immerse yourself in a unique experience in Medellín. Discover the natural beauty of the city at the Botanical Garden!

  • What to do?
  • ➣ See a 110-year-old tree.
  • ➣ Rest in its spaces.
  • ➣ Take amazing photographs.
  • ➣ Have a picnic with your friends.
  • ➣ Enjoy a lake with many species.
  • ➣ Enter the Butterfly House.
  • ➣ See relics from Antioquia.
  • ➣ Discover exotic plants.
